# Month 5: Build With AI Hackathon

Period: Sep 20 - Oct 20, 2026
Phase: Growth

## Strategic Intent
Month 5 creates a major proof point: GDGoC BUK can organize, partner, build, document, and attract university-wide attention.

## Main Objectives
- Run the formal Build with AI Hackathon.
- Activate SUG BUK partnership.
- Celebrate mid-session progress.
- Publish a blog or press release.

## Execution Notes
- Define judging criteria early: usefulness, execution, demo quality, and learning.
- Pair beginners with more experienced members where possible.
- Capture photos, stories, project links, and quotes during the event.

## Milestones
- 30+ hackathon participants.
- Five or more projects submitted.
- Internal showcase completed.
- University media coverage secured.

## Risks To Watch
- Logistics overwhelming the core team.
- Projects not being documented after demos.
