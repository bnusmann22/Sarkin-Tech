# Month 6: Project Polish And Impact Draft

Period: Oct 20 - Nov 20, 2026
Phase: Showcase

## Strategic Intent
Month 6 turns raw work into presentable impact by polishing projects, preparing Demo Day, and documenting the story of Session 1.

## Main Objectives
- Review and polish cluster projects.
- Prepare Demo Day workshops.
- Draft the community impact report.
- Identify potential Year 2 leads.

## Execution Notes
- Give every project clear feedback: product, technical quality, demo narrative, and next steps.
- Start the impact report with numbers, stories, partner wins, and photos.
- Let potential future leads shadow planning meetings.

## Milestones
- All projects demo-ready.
- Demo Day prep workshops completed.
- Impact report drafted.
- Year 2 core team candidates identified.

## Risks To Watch
- Waiting too late to prepare demos.
- Ignoring succession until the final month.
