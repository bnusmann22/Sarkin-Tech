# Month 7: Demo Day And Public Showcase

Period: Nov 20 - Dec 1, 2026
Phase: Showcase

## Strategic Intent
Month 7 makes the impact visible to the whole university and gives members a moment of recognition for the work they have done.

## Main Objectives
- Host Demo Day.
- Showcase live projects.
- Recognize contributors and partners.
- Publish media recaps.

## Execution Notes
- Design Demo Day like a story: why GDGoC matters, what members built, and where the community goes next.
- Invite every partner early and give them visible appreciation.
- Prepare backup demo videos for projects in case live demos misbehave.

## Milestones
- 100+ Demo Day attendees.
- 10+ projects shown.
- Awards and recognition completed.
- University-wide media coverage achieved.

## Risks To Watch
- Demos failing without backup plans.
- Recognition missing quiet but important contributors.
