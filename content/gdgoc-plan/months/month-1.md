# Month 1: Launch Foundation

Period: May 20 - Jun 20, 2026
Phase: Launch

## Strategic Intent
Month 1 is about making GDGoC BUK visible again and giving the community a clear public signal that something serious, welcoming, and consistent has started.

## Main Objectives
- Assemble the core team of 8-10 reliable members.
- Launch or refresh social media presence across Instagram, WhatsApp, and X.
- Secure the Faculty of Computing partnership.
- Announce the session theme publicly: GDGoC dey for you.

## Execution Notes
- Treat the kickoff event as a trust-building moment, not only an announcement.
- Keep the onboarding call practical: roles, rhythm, communication channels, and expectations.
- Document every partnership conversation and follow up within 48 hours.

## Milestones
- Kick-off event completed.
- Core team onboarded.
- Two partnership MOUs signed.
- 50+ new social followers.

## Risks To Watch
- Too much excitement without clear ownership.
- Partnership talks staying informal without written next steps.
