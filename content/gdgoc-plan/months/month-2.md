# Month 2: Campus Reach Expansion

Period: Jun 20 - Jul 20, 2026
Phase: Launch

## Strategic Intent
Month 2 moves GDGoC BUK beyond the immediate tech audience and starts proving that the community is useful across faculties.

## Main Objectives
- Expand reach to NACOS and NUESA.
- Host the first broad public orientation.
- Register at least 100 members.
- Make the theme familiar across campus.

## Execution Notes
- Make the orientation beginner-friendly and story-driven.
- Use flyers, class reps, WhatsApp groups, and partner communities together.
- Capture registrations in one clean source of truth.

## Milestones
- GDGoC dey for you orientation hosted.
- NACOS joint session completed.
- 100 registered members reached.
- Three or more faculties represented.

## Risks To Watch
- Registration without actual engagement.
- Messaging that still sounds too technical for non-tech students.
