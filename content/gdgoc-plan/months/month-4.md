# Month 4: Depth And Visibility

Period: Aug 20 - Sep 20, 2026
Phase: Growth

## Strategic Intent
Month 4 deepens the learning culture while keeping the wider campus aware that GDGoC BUK is active and valuable.

## Main Objectives
- Deepen cluster learning.
- Host guest speakers.
- Push mid-session visibility.
- Run a member survey.

## Execution Notes
- Invite alumni and industry guests who can give practical advice.
- Start cluster projects early enough for review and iteration.
- Use member spotlights to humanize the community.

## Milestones
- Three or more speakers hosted.
- Every cluster has a project.
- Member spotlight series published.
- 150+ total members reached.

## Risks To Watch
- Speaker sessions becoming passive lectures.
- Not acting on survey feedback quickly.
